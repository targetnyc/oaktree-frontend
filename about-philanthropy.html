In 2015, Oaktree commemorated its 20th anniversary by launching a special initiative, Our Communities Matter (OCM), to formalize the Company’s long-standing tradition of supporting our communities.

Through this program, Oaktree and its employees are able to dedicate their time and resources to numerous meaningful and much-needed community initiatives across the globe.
